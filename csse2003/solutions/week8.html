Full size image

Many variations on this solution are also valid. To list a few:
  • We could have the TelephoneExchange as an enum, which would then be directly accessible, instead of a static field of the Person. We could also have tied it in to the Village, so it is unique to the Village instance, and accessible through Village.INSTANCE.
  • If you were to follow the Mediator pattern exactly as described in the lecture notes, you could make interfaces for these two classes.
  • Modify the send/receive methods to also take in the ID of the sender. That way, the recipient can easily send a reply if needed.
