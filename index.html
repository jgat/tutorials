tutorials

A collection of content that I've prepared for classroom tutorials I deliver at the University of Queensland.

Where I can, I'm including binaries for ease of use, and raw source for ease of modification (e.g. LaTeX documents will generally have both the source .tex and generated .pdf files, as well as a makefile).

Available content includes materials that I generated myself, and some additional material which was already publicly available during the offering of the course.

To request the addition of materials, or to point out a mistake, create an issue, or contact me directly.

Courses

Tutorial material is made available from a subset of the course offerings I have tutored:

  • CSSE1001, Semesters (1 2011; 2 2011; 1 2012; 2 2012; 1 2013; 2 2013)
  • MATH1061, Semesters (2 2012; 1 2013; 2 2013)
  • DECO2800, Semester 2 2013
  • CSSE2003, Semester 2 2012
  • MATH1051, Semester 1 2012
  • MATH1050, Semester 2 2011

Content from offerings listed above that don't appear here are either because I didn't generate any electronic resources for teaching, I haven't bothered committing them yet, or the material is unsuitable for public access (e.g. exercise solutions).
